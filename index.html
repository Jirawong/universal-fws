our STORY

Evolving from The California Wine Company with 26 years of experience,

Out comes the Universal Fine Wine and Spirit Co. Our humble story began 26 years ago when our founder, Charles Huang, de cided to introduce the taste of California to Thailand during the time when the wine market was predominantly French and Italian producers. Under his guidance our portfolio continued to grow to include some of the ﬁnest producers and most historic brands from the world-famous Napa Valley such as Duckhorn, Shafer, Caymus, Colgin, Chateau Montelena, Far Niente, Jo Phelps, Schramsberg, and many more.
