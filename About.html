UNIVERSAL FINE WINE & SPIRITS

OUR STORY

Evolving from The California Wine Company with 26 years of experience, out comes the Universal Fine Wine and Spirit Co. Our humble story began 26 years ago when our founder, Charles Huang, decided to introduce the taste of California to Thailand during the time when the wine market was predominantly French and Italian producers. Under his guidance, our portfolio continued to grow to include some of the finest producers and most historic brands from the world-famous Napa Valley such as Duckhorn, Shafer, Caymus, Colgin, Chateau Montelena, Far Niente, Joseph Phelps, Schramsberg, and many more.

Our Entrepreneurial spirit did not end with California selections as we continue to grow to include major brands outside the US such as Marques de Murrieta, Schloss Johannisburg, Mission Estate, Maison Sichel, and many more.

Even in the hands of the second generation, our Entrepreneurial spirit does not end as we continue to evolve with our clientâ€™s needs by adding craft spirits into our portfolio.

As time passes and the company continues to evolve, one thing remains constant from day one which is our desire to best service our clients and partners as one of the leading importers and distributors of wine and spirits in Thailand.

OUR MISSION

To share our passion for wine with the Thai market and offer our customers a wide range of top-quality wines from all over the world.

We work with hotels, restaurants, retailers and wine aficionados throughout the country as well as conducting wine tasting events, organize dinners with leading wine personalities, vintners and winemakers from key wine producing regions to help support and educate our clients.

We strive to deliver the best quality wines and service to our clients as well as share our knowledge to help expand the market as a whole.
